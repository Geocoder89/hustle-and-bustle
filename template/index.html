Company overview

About Us

Across the world, bars and night clubs are symbolic features of every city’s social life. With one of the world’s fastest rising urbanization rates, Africa’s cities are becoming hotspots for single professionals, young adults, tourists, and business travelers who want to connect and have a great time over drinks, food and music. Generally, have a feel of Lifestyle!...
The growing spending power on the continent, its overwhelmingly, young population and huge appetite for fun, relaxation and partying makes the nightclub business an attractive prospect for entrepreneurs and investors. With an estimated 6 million strong population, 60% of which is made up of young people, Abuja is a large market for the nightclub business.
Despite the dominance of Major Nightclubs like Moscow Underground and Crave by Cubana alongside other, bars and Lounges in Abuja nightlife scene, funlovers, and party goers are always eager to new experience as it may present itself. It is against this backdrop that the founders and sponsors of Hotwire Partners Limited and Cubana Group Limited established the world class brand HUSTLE AND BUSTLE NIGHTCLUB AND CAFÉ LIMITED under all applicable Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria to address the existing gap in the Abuja nightclub scene.
